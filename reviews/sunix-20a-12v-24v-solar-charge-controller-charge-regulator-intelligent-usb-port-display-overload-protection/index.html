Does a lot for the money

For the money, this unit does much more than an item you could build yourself. The display, however, is not backlit, and is inverted (black on white) from the unit shown. Also setting various parameters (like setting when the load can go ON/OFF) is very confusing; I will try to update with instructions after I get some experience with it. This is neither a boost controller nor an MPPT; your panel has to have more voltage than the battery. Make sure you get the appropriate model for Li-Ion or lead acid, as they are separate models!
