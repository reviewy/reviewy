The Altura Photo AP-C1001 Speedlite for Canon is a powerful dedicated flash that provides you greater flexibility over the standard pop-up flash. The wireless flash trigger set allows you to take the flash off camera to add dimension and direction to your light.Perfect for portrait photography and great for photographing large groups, special occasions, and weddings.

Features: Backed with Digital Goja 90-Day 100% Satisfaction Guarantee

Includes 1-Year Altura Photo Limited Warranty

Altura Photo Professional E-TTL Flash AP-C1001 - Powerful flash performance with a guide no. of 68(m) - Dedicated flash that works in both the automatic and manual modes - Large, easy to read rear backlit display - Features a built-in optical sensor for off-camera flash capability - Super fast 4 second recycling time - NOTE: This flash does not support rear curtain and high-speed sync

Wireless Flash Trigger Set with Remote Shutter Function Simply slide the transmitter onto your camera’s hot-shoe, and your flash onto the included receiver - Operating Distance: Up to 30 meters (100 feet) - Flash sync speed up to 1/250s - Serves also as a wireless shutter release - Includes: 1 transmitter and 1 receiver, camera shutter trigger cable, and batteries -NOTE: This trigger does not support E-TTL mode
