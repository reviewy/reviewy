    Product Description

Think Tank Photo Red Whips Adjustable Cable Ties (Pack of 10) enable quick, neat cable management by keeping cords together and eliminating tangles. They are also useful for luggage identification, thanks to their bright red color, and have numerous other possible applications. These durable elastic wraps have a barrel lock cord closure for easy size adjustment. Wonderful Widget Wrangles One’s Wayward Wires

I’m a musician and a DJ. Consequently I have lots of wires to manage. The thing about wires is this: THEY LOVE TO GET TANGLED UP WITH THEMSELVES & OTHER WIRES. You have to manage them or they’ll get out of hand in a hurry. Think Tank has devised an ingenious way of corralling wires. You wrap the red fabric covered elastic bands around the cables, pull the head trough the loop, then squeeze the button on the black thingy to tighten up the grip on the wires. Done! Easy to manage cables and wires both large and small. And the bright red makes these easy to see. If you want to manage your cords, this is a fine solution for that need.

so they are easily visible as the luggage arrives

We used the “whips” on our luggage during a recent three-week trip to Southeast Asia. Untie the knot at the end, slip off the “clip,” loop the whip over the luggage handle, reattach the clip and slide it towards the handle. The strands are stiff enough to stand up, so they are easily visible as the luggage arrives. Also worked on backpacks and carry-ons.Funny story: a fellow traveler asked what the “antenna” were on the backpacks my wife and I were using. We told him they were part of a GPS system so we could keep track of each other’s location. Sigh.
