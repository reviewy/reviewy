Product Description

These are 48 kids microscope slides of entomology, botany and mammology specimens included. The slides are made of plastics without sharp edges and are safe for kids. This slide set is good for educational purpose for kids, entry-level students or home school programs. Some slides (like insects …) are designed for the observation of stereo microscopes that come with top and bottom lights. Compound microscopes may not give you the best microscopy images for those slides.

In red color, you can find butterfly wing, butterfly antenna, butterfly leg, locust wing, locust antenna, locust leg, dragonfly wing, dragonfly abdomen, dragonfly leg, honeybee wing, honeybee antenna and honeybee leg.

In green color, you will see celery leaf, corn stem, lotus root, cabbage leaf, pumpkin ovary, ginger root, onion epidermis, burdock root, carrot root, cucumber ovary, sponge gourd stem and potato starch.

In yellow color, there are dandelion fuzz, carnation stem, tulip pollen, laver, lily pollen, camellia pollen, sunflower pollen, bamboo cane, phlox leaf, pine tree stem, veins of holy leaf and agar.

In blue color, there exist goldfish scale, sardine scale, plankton egg, canary feather, rabbit hair, cat hair, horse hair, cow hair, sheep hair, fowl feather, duck feather and pigeon feather.
