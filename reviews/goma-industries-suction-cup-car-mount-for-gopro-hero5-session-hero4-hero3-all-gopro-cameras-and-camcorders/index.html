Product Reviews

A Great Mount

I was looking for a windshield suction cup mount that was smaller than the GoPro branded mount and the GOMA mount met all my expectations. It is definitely smaller, but the suction cup is still quite strong. It is well made and the smaller size makes it less obtrusive when mounted to my windshield. The only complaint, and a minor one, is the size of the faux leather storage bag. It is a bit too small and it takes a bit of wrangling to get the mount to fit inside. No biggie and it shouldn’t deter anyone from purchasing this mount. My order arrived on time in a padded envelope. I wouldn’t hesitate to order from this company again should the need arise.

Great Mount for Indoor Skydiving

My wife is a competition indoor skydiver, and two of the tunnels she trains at have really dodgy camera mounts for people to use. The level and angle aren’t ideal, that part of the tunnel glass is dirty or “foggy”, or (worst of all) the mount isn’t secure and the camera can slip and tilt whilst she’s in the tunnel training (losing valuable training footage).The GOMA suction cup mount is absolutely brilliant for solving this problem. She can place it at the spot of her choosing, which is great because sometimes she wants to film from different angles/levels. The mount stays firmly in place on the tunnel plexiglass wall (and there’s a lot of vibration from the fans). The design of the mount allows her to position the camera lens right up against the glass, which help prevents distortion and reflection issues.
