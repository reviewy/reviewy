Other than that, you will also need to adjust the tension of the bolts with the allen keys that are included. the built-in level definitely helps, just attach one of the holes first and don’t tighten all the way, then you can pivot it with the arm extended to make it level. the arm itself feels very sturdy, I’ve seen much heavier arms in hotels with smaller tvs that feel real flimsy, this one feels very solid and it’s not a small tv.
