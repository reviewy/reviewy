    Product Description

Product Description The XY3 is a 3rd generation Bluetooth item finder that lets you use your phone to find lost or misplaced things. Attach an XY3 to the things you lose the most - your keys, the remote, or the kids’ backpacks - and find them in seconds. Lost your phone? The XY3 can make it ring, even on silent.

The XY3 is small and lightweight enough to be attached to almost anything. It’s a key finder, luggage tracker, wallet locator, pet finder, purse tracker - the possibilities are endless! It connects to your phone or tablet via a Bluetooth Low Energy signal and uses your phone’s GPS to track its location.

The XY3 uses a free app, XY Find It, which is compatible with devices running iOS 8.1+ or Android 4.4+. Compatible iPhones include iPhone 4S, iPhone 5, iPhone 5C, iPhone 5S, iPhone 6, iPhone 6 Plus, iPhone 6S, iPhone 6S Plus, iPhone SE, iPhone 7, and iPhone 7 Plus. Compatible iPads include the 3rd gen and 4th gen iPad, iPad Air, iPad Mini, and iPad Pro. Some compatible Android devices include Samsung Galaxy S5, S6, S6, S7, Samsung Note 4, Note 5, HTC One (M8+) and Google Pixel.
