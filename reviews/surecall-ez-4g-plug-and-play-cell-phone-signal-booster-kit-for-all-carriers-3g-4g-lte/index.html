I Have Finally Found What Works For Me

WHY I LOOKED FOR THIS TYPE OF DEVICE: I live in an outlying, rural area with only incredibly slow DSL or satelite as options for internet. I have a Sprint MiFi Hotspot device (Franklin Wireless r850) with unlimited internet, but the signal to my house is spotty at best. An average measured RSSI of -82 dB is what I was getting with the hotspot placed in the best location within my house. This allowed for speeds of (again, measured average) 2.5 Mbps down and 1.6 Mbps up (compared to my old AT&T DSL of 1.3 Mbps down and 0.2 Mbps up). Unfortunately, the signal was so bad that it would often drop out. So I knew I needed a cell signal booster.WHAT I TRIED: I purchased another indoor signal booster from a competitor to SureCall, but it didn’t do much. I contacted that competitor (great customer service) and was told that their equipment does not work well with some of Sprint’s ranges. They actually recommended looking at SureCall’s devices.MY RESULTS WITH SURECALL: I purchased EZ 4G booster kit and, upon arrival, installed the receiver at the same spot where my hotspot received the best signal. I placed the repeater downstairs (about 30’ away) and my RSSI went from -82 dB to -52 dB!!! I thought that this was a fluke, so I ran continual tests over the past few days on the signal and the speeds. The signal ranged from -55 dB to -51 dB the entire time and I ran speed tests via testmy.net every 5 minutes for 4 days. My current average speeds over this time period are 5.1 Mbps down and 2.2 Mbps up. This is a significant improvement for me!Obviously, everyone will get different results, but I am super satisfied with this purchase.
