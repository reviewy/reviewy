Product Reviews

Not bad, but had initial issue…

Nice product at the sale price. I wouldn’t have bought at anywhere near retail however. One issue I had out of the box deserves comment. If you have a prior Google account, when you sign in it will sync with your prior bookmarks and extensions. I had an immediate issue with chrome crashing every 30 seconds! What worked for me was to “browse as a Guest” from the login screen then select the “customize and control Google” under the “x” in the browser (those three vertical dots thing) then selecting “settings”. Next, select the blue “about Chrome OS” in the top center of the screen. Now hit the “check for and apply updates” box. After a few minutes, it will load and you will be prompted to restart the unit. I guess my problem was that my Lenovo shipped with an earlier version of Chrome that wasn’t compatible with my synced extensions and was causing a loop of chrome crashing. I am glad I stumbled upon this before I decided to ship it back!
