Excellent Television Station Reception, Superb Image Quality and Well Featured

I’m exactly 50 miles in a straight line northwest of the Houston TV transmitting towers and using the exact same antenna for my older Panasonic HDTV, this Samsung UHD television brought in twice as many stations and without any pixel breakup like my 7 year old Panasonic HDTV does. A few of the new stations are the ones that transmit 4 channels on one frequency channel, so I can’t really count them in the doubling of stations. It would require some note taking to get the exact number of distinct new stations I’ve gained. But I can certainly say I’m getting a lot more stations with much clearer solid signals without the pixel breakup on some channels I had previously. And I’m using one of those inexpensive $20 to $25 flat amplified antennas lying flat on the top of a chest near a window. So I’d say the signal reception of this Samsung UHD television is absolutely exceptional.Of course, all of the television stations are still broadcasting in HD, but the video image is still nothing short of outstanding. My wife and I never even noticed the curved screen while we were watching the news. I wasn’t sure I would like a curved screen, but the curve is quite subtle and not overdone like it is on my 27 inch Samsung computer monitor. At first glance, you wouldn’t notice this television screen is curved.The audio volume is not very loud, but it is clean sound with no detectable distortion. We run it at full volume and while we can hear it adequately across a large open living area, it’s not very impressive when watching an action movie. I just got a Sony BDPS6700 4K Upscaling receiver with UHD pass-through. I have enough speakers left over from previous upgrades to make a full 5.1 surround system. I already have a Sony Blu-ray player from Amazon with UHD upscaling. I tested the 4K upscaling last night with a recently released movie and while I can’t verbally describe how much the image is improved, the detail is incredible. I would need an identical system side-by-side to assess the upscaling improvement. What I can say is the image is excellent with or without the upscaling.But the real proof came from actual 4K video shot with a Samsung camera that I gave to my daughter. The detail and resolution are just awesome. I almost never use the word awesome, but the video files my daughter sent to me were so incredible, it was the only word suitable to describe the quality. I wish it was possible to show the video to you so you could witness how amazing it is. Great buy!

Great TV! The picture is awesome with pretty much every media put thru it. It does a great job of upscaling old tv and movies. There are a few different modes for the tv to display, standard, natural, movie, etc. On standard mode, it makes everything look “live”. Tho the picture is still fantastic, I don’t care much for it, so a trick is to put it in movie mode (then go into the setup and amp up the sharpness) and it will look more like what you may be used to. I suppose the sound is okay, but if you’re not sporting a surround sound system, then you are only getting half the experience anyway. My only complaint would be the remote. While sleek, it’s a little too simplistic. Just a few more buttons for for basic playback functions would be nice, rather than selecting FF, RW, Play, etc, from a menu. This becomes an issue if you choose to stream video from the built in apps.
