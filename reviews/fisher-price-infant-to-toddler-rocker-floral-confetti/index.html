Perfect for baby with a big brother

Great value for the price. Easy to assemble and the fabric seat portion removes easily for washing. I keep this in the kitchen so my 4-month-old baby can be near me while I cook. She’s still young enough to love the vibrating feature and this keeps her elevated enough to be able to look around at her surroundings. This chair does seem a wide for an infant, but I intentionally got something that her 3-year-old brother could also sit in, since he was inevitably going to try it out and I didn’t want to worry about him breaking it! So far it’s holding up great.
