Product Reviews

Freakishly Bright!

This is an excellent flashlight: EXTREMELY bright. It has five different modes: high, medium, low, strobe and SOS. You click the button on the bottom to change modes. It switches to off between each mode. You can also zoom by sliding the lens cap forward. The zoom is really good and controllable unlike some other flashlights. Zoomed to its narrowest setting you can clearly see the square LED array. It’s the perfect size and fits nicely into your hand. It appears to be pretty tough. I’ve dropped several times with no harm. Unlike incandescent bulbs you can’t break the LED by dropping it. You get a whopping four hours on the high setting and much more on the lower settings.CONSIt’s kind of a hassle to switch through all the modes to get to the mode you want, having to switch to off between each mode. That’s ten clicks to cycle all the way through.It takes three AAA cells which is an odd number. For my rechargeable batteries you have to charge in pairs; this means I have to leave a battery out when recharging and remember that it has to be recharged the next time I have to recharge an odd number of batteries.Don’t look directly into the flashlight up close: it will temporarily blind you and could damage your eyes!The pros definitely outweigh the cons. I highly recommend this flashlight.
