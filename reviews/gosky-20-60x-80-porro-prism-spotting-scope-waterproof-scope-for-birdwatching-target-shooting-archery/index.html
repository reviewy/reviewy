Excellent Spotting Scope!!!

I researched a lot of spotting scopes before settling on this one. The description for this GoSky scope did not list the glass used and the numbers didn’t add up right to me. I found the identical scope on ebay with two different branding names both from china like this one and all for about the same price (except the GoSky includes the digi-adapter). Both those ebay scopes have BK7 glass and the following numbers: 1200 grams weight, field of view 94ft@1000yds@20x and 53ft@1000yds@60x, eye relief 18-15mm and exit pupil 4-1.33mm. These numbers looked correct for this size scope. Based on the ebay info for the others I decided to order this GoSky spotting scope assuming its numbers would really match the others.I’m glad I did! I found the weight to be 1200 grams just like the others so I believe the glass to be BK7 and I also believe the numbers to match the other ebay scopes. I also found the image quality to be excellent. It’s crystal clear at 20x and the images are also quite clear at 60x when using the digi-adapter with my cell phone which takes pictures using voice commands. Remember that atmospheric effects (like heat waves) on image quality are amplified at higher powers. This affects all scopes.So I find this GoSky to be an excellent, nicely featured, well built spotting scope. The price makes it simply unbeatable.
