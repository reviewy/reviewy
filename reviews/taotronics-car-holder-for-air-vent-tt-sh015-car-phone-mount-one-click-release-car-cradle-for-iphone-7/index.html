Perfect Solution For Safely Using Your Cell Phone In A Car, With No Damage To Your Phone Or Vehicle Interior.

Do you have a good way to keep your cell phone readily accessible while driving? I used to keep my phone on the passenger seat (only works if you do not have a passenger), or in one of the dash or console compartments (phone slides around and sometimes out onto the floor). I had quickly realized that I needed a method for securely mounting my phone in the car. I have tried nearly all of the various mounting methods (suction cup, Velcro patches, weighted bean bags, magnetic, etc.). None were really satisfactory. The suction cup type sometimes dropped my cell phone, and left hard to remove marks on my windshield. The sticky glue of Velcro patches has damaged my dashboard. The weighted bean bags still slipped, and did not securely hold my phone. And, there are very few places on modern cars to use a magnetic mount.I believe I have finally found a solution! The TaoTronics Car Air Vent Phone Mount Holder (TT-SH015) fits all of my requirements for a phone holder. It mounts, and unmounts, quickly, easily, and securely to a dash air vent blade. It uses silicone rubber pads (does not damage any part of the car in any way). A two position lever (one positon for thin air vent blades, and the other position for thicker blades), makes this holder usable in just about any car air vent (horizontal fins or vertical). It holds the phone itself securely to the mount with grooved rubber pads, that ratchet and lock your phone as you push the holder arms together (works with or without phone cases, and causes no damage to either your phone or case). The ratchet holder releases the phone easily with the push of a button on the mount (using only one hand). The position of the phone is quickly adjustable to just about any angle or position (including vertical and horizontal). The mount then can be easily locked with the turn of a knob for tightening, and locking the position desired.So, how does the mount work in actual driving? Excellent! I really liked the position of the phone while using this mount. It placed the phone just to the right of the steering wheel (when using the center air vent), and in close proximity while driving (more easily accessible than any of my car’s dash controls or audio system controls). An incoming call can be answered by a touch of the phone, and another touch to place the phone in speaker mode, allowing total hands-free operation after that. This allows quick, and easy, one hand operation of the phone, and minimal eye movement from the road (for much improved safety).I sometimes use my cell phone as a GPS locator (map), and for instructions in finding a location, and using Google Maps to monitor traffic. This TaoTronics mount puts the phone in an excellent position (close and under the windshield) for excellent reception of the GPS satellites signals. I have had trouble with GPS reception with my cell phone in other positions in the car, as the satellite signals are shielded by the car’s metal parts. This unit puts the phone in the optimum position for good reception.The phone mount appears to be constructed of ABS plastic (strong), and the quality of construction is excellent (very sturdy). The finish is nice. The instructions say to place the mount, with the lever down, to the bottom air vent fin for the maximum stability. I purposely placed the mount with the lever up, and on air vent fins other than the bottom one, and all positions seemed to work equally well for me. I used the mount and phone in 3 different cars, and it worked very well in all (2011 Honda Accord, 2011 Honda CVC, and a 1997 Honda Civic). I used the mount with an iPhone 5s, an iPhone 6, and a Samsung Galaxy S5, all with cases, and without. All were held securely in the mount. The mount is stated to hold phones with a width from 1.97” to 3.70”. I will definitely be using this phone mount, and purchasing another.
