A good and reliable memory card…

I have 4 of these for my pro Canon video camcorder and use it for my business. The cards have been very reliable. A few times, I could not erase the card - the computer would tell me that it was write protected and could not be erased. The lock on the card was not engaged. I engaged it and tried again, getting the now expected ‘write protected’ message. Then I turn the lock off again and put it back in the slot - this time, I could scrub the card and have it empty and ready to go for more video shooting. This has only happened a few times out of many uses (2 of the 4 cards maybe?), and I do not know why it happens at all. After the maneuver I described in this post, I am able to proceed as planned and clear the card for the next use. I don’t know if this happens randomly with other cards or not. Overall, I would still recommend this card for the reliability I have experienced so far.
