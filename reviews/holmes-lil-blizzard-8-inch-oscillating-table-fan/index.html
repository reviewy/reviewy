An intense blow from a small desk-top fan.’

I had a Vornado before I bought this one. Even on high, it was not much of a breeze. I was happy when it died. This fan is just the opposite. On low it produces a rather strong blow. The high setting will blow your hair back, and both settings blow papers off the desk. It is almost too strong a breeze. There are only two speeds, the reason I didn’t give it 5 stars. There should be three, one a bit lower than this unit’s lowest setting. The oscillating switch is very difficult to work. Still, if you want a small fan that produces a very strong blow, this one is for you. I read all the 1 star reviews and I did not have the problems that the reviewers stated. I don’t think it is too loud at all. I haven’t used it for long yet, so time will tell if they were correct in their assessment that it failed early on. I kept the shipping boxes, just in case.

Not Too Crazy About the Oscillating Function

Maybe you’re not going to find a 5 star fan for less than $20.If you’re looking for a fan that blows straight ahead and does it well, this is your fan. Seems to be well balanced and moves air nicely. It should last a long time. If you’re looking for a fan you plan on using the Oscillating feature regularly, keep looking, this is not it. The oscillating movement is jerky like it’s hanging up on something inside the housing, in both directions. That means it’s either a bad design, or poor assembly. Either way, it probably won’t last too long. I’m not interested in taking it apart to see what’s wrong. My primary use is blow straight ahead.
