Product Reviews

Whole-heartedly recommended by my wife

My wife loves these earbuds because they not only sound good and do a great job of blocking outside noise, but they’re also magnetic and hook together to form a necklace so that she doesn’t lose them. Plus, she likes that the rubber hook things which are supposed to help hold them in your ear can be removed, because she doesn’t like those things and they stay in fine without them.These come with three different sizes of rubber earbud and hook attachments so you’re bound to find one that fits your ears. There is also a little carry pouch which she hasn’t used and a micro-USB charging cable which is very short, like only a few inches long. She hasn’t used that either as her phone uses a micro-USB cable so she just uses that one. The box it comes in is also pretty nice. It’s standard cardboard tan color, but has silver reflective lettering for the WIRELESS STEREO HEADPHONES text, rounded corners, and easy-to-open clam-shell design.She took these earbuds with her on our recent trip to L.A. and she loved them as they were crisp and clear and blocked out the sound of the car and the talking that my friend and I were doing in the front seat. Today she used them at work and loved them there as well, so she whole-heartedly recommends them.
