Good cable sleeve but not exactly the best

The PRO’s:- The product looks nice, black is obviously a good color to make them, not question about that.- The product feels nice, you would think it would attract hair if you have pets but I have cats and dogs in and out of my room and so far they look very nice and have little to no hair attached to them, a big plus to me.- It comes with 4 sleeves (depending on the variant you purchase) which is an adequate amount, I don’t see myself needing any more than that honestly. As of now I have 2 of the sleeves running up my desk for my computer peripherals (mouse, keyboard, HDMI, DVI, audio cables, etc.) and the other 2 sleeves are covering the cables that run to my sub-woofer (that being the cables for the 2 desktop units that run to it as well as power cable)- Potentially protects the cables from heat somewhat. Now I’m not sure if there’s any science behind this, it simply gives me peace of mind that all the cables I believe are protected in this sleeve as all my cables run near the radiator- High quality zipper, I have yanked and pulled these so hard to stretch around my cables I swear I thought that the cables would burst right out of the zipper but it holds up surprisingly well.That’s about it for the pro’s, I mean it’s a cable sleeve, what more is there to sayThe CON’S:- The sleeves in my opinion are a bit small, I wish they were bigger to accommodate for all my cables. They could either offer it as a separate option or just implement it throughout the product line. Either way, zipping these up is ridiculously hard. All I’m going to say is there’s not much wiggle room, they really don’t stretch as much as you would think.- Unlike a lot of cable management sleeves out there you cannot branch off any of the cables unless you did something like cut a hole in them but I don’t believe JOTO would want you doing that and if they have a warranty that would most likely void it. Not a problem so much for me but if you’re looking to do that, please find a different cable management sleeve as this will not cut it for you- Miss aligned zippers, that’s all that really needs to be said but I’ll go on because it just annoys me. If you’re looking to zip it all the way up, good luck, for all of my cable sleeves they don’t line up properly. Doesn’t really mess it up, doesn’t even look that bad as it’s just a cable sleeve but close up it just annoys me. Either the left or right side of the zipper will stick out a bit, and I’ve chosen to accept that.- Wish they added more lengths, 40” is a bit too big for me, and 19” is a bit too small. Now I know they can’t cater to my needs, they go with the majority and I guess that’s what the majority has but a few more options would have been a welcome addition.- Taking these off are a PAIN, honestly the most annoying part. I had to route a usb extender for my new usb hub and it took maybe a half an hour no lie at all to get it right. Once the cables are in there you will NOT get any leeway, the cables are in there for good (at least with my arrangement of a million cables in one sleeve) so you have to mess around with it until it’s just right and then zip them both up hoping to god you have not messed anything up. In the end it looks very nice and neat, but what a pain to get it that way. It seems the zipper never wants to zip up, it’s just a hair too high to be zipped up and you have to start all over again.———————————–In the end it’s a cable sleeve, nothing more, nothing less. However, it’s not the perfect god given gift of a cable sleeve as some reviewers would have you believe. That’s not to say it’s not a good cable sleeve, it’s a damn good one, and it looks professional. You could go and get those ones that look like a snake or whatever made of plastic and I’m sure they’re better, but do you really want that thing hanging from your desk? However, I’m not here to try and trick you into buying this product, it’s the one I bought because I was on a time constraint. If you’re reading this, you’re probably not as it’s extremely long by now, longer than I wanted it to be.
