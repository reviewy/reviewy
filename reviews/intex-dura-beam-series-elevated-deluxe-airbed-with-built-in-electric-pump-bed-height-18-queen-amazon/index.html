Airpockets are amazing

I have purchased a couple of Intex airbeds already - knowing that I can depend on the quality of their products, I bought this airbed for when guests come over. A few different things that I noticed were the packaging and the actual airbed itself.This item came in a different packaging which I would assume is some type of Amazon special packaging. It’s not a retail box but a brown box that notes what’s inside. It didn’t make a big difference to me since I already knew what I was going to get.The airbed itself was very comfortable. The air pockets are something new that the other Intex airbeds I own don’t have. It’s a small feature but makes a world of difference in regards to overall comfort. The size and height is perfect, my sister is 5’1” and fits on the bed sprawled out with room to spare. 18” is high enough off the ground that it’s not too awkwardly low to the floor or too high off the ground. It of course comes with the built-in pump which is convenient. The little compartment to roll the cord back into is not only aesthetically pleasing but adds a level of safety. I’m extremely clumsy and can see myself tripping over this if it wasn’t tucked away somewhere nicely.Overall a great quality bed, I would highly recommend. It also comes with a very convenient carrying case.
