Product Reviews

Excellent battery

I have used these batteries for almost two years now. I use my GoPro on my bike every ride and I average 5 rides per week with the average ride being 33 miles. So the batteries get a lot of use. After about 1 1⁄2 years they were noticeably weaker and I just replaced them. But they outlasted the genuine GoPro battery which began to swell up and wouldn’t fit in the camera any more. So at a fraction of the price, these are an excellent deal.

Gopro batteries

Great gopro battery and even better price. Real gopro batteries cost double the price.

Good value and performance

I have four of these batteries. They all give me about 90-plus minutes of recording time (unless wifi is on, which shortens them up considerably as it does the GoPro OEM battery). The only drawback I’ve seen is the GoPro phone app doesn’t work right when using these batteries. For my use, this is not a big deal and they still perform well for the price. But if you have to use the smartphone app as a remote, be advised that you may need to stick to GoPro original batteries.
