Just Be Aware Before you Buy

Firstly this product works as advertised. It works as a lightning cable and give you a little extra power. What could be better describes is how much a little extra power is.With what I thought was a full charge on the cable it took my phone from about 45% battery to about 52% battery in 21 minutes and then the charge was empty. I made sure i didn’t use my phone while charging to get things accurate.This isn’t a full battery pack, so if that is what you want don’t buy it.Also I don’t think the battery charges while your phone is hooked into it, I could be wrong, but it took me a minute to figure that out, but it seemed that when I had my phone hooked up to it when I first got it and then tried to use the battery feature nothing happened until I had it hooked up to charge without anything connected. This could be better explained in the instructions if true.It is well designed, and works as advertised, but I think 3 hours of extra battery life may be stretching it a little bit, even in standby mode. Overall if you need another lightning cable and want to spend an extra 10 bucks you can get a little extra batery life for a little price, just be aware that is is a “little” bit of extra battery life.
