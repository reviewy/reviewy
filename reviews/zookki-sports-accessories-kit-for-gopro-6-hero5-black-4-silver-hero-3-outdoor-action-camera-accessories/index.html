Easiest 5 stars I’ve ever given

I have to give this 5 stars. When I first saw the price and saw some of the reviews I was very skeptical. Less than $15 for all that stuff?! I was convinced that it was cheap and probably didn’t fit right. One was slightly loose (gator clip) so that might cause some movement when clipped to something and it’s jarred hard enough. Bottom line: this is the easiest 5 star rating I’ve ever given. Buy it ASAP before they raise the price of run out.
