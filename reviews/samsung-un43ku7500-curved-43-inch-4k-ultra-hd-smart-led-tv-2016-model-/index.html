Other than the above mentioned pairing with the new hopper 3 from dish (which did work with an extra 60 seconds effort) setup is a dream. Selecting letters for ids and passwords is super fast and easy with this little remote and the interface and crisp response time.AESTHETICS: She’s a beut!!!
