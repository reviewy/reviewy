Super Portable & Accurate

 The hanger is a very handy thing to have for anything under 40Kg. Here is my thought about the different aspect of it:Size:This portable scale is very compact. The hanger has a place behind the scale so the whole thing can be fit in any bag or even a packet.Units:The scale can show the weight in Lb, JIN, Oz, and Kg.Battery:It takes 2x AAA batteries. It last a long time I used the hanger a lot in the past couple of weeks and I still have not seen the low battery sign.Backlight:The scale has backlight which turn on with the device and turn off if no button is pressed after a while. This is perfect specially if the environment is dark as it makes it super easy to see the number.Locking function:The locking function can be enabled/disabled. It locks the number after a couple of second after something is hanged from the hook. This function is good if you want to have the number locked on the screen even after you take the weigh off the hook. This function works hand in hand with the LED above the screen that blinks as the scale is weighing but turn solid when it locks the number and you see a lock sign on the LCD as well. But if you are adding weigh or the item is such that the weighing takes a while (e.g. if the item is swinging for a while, etc.) to show it’s stable weight you might want to turn this off. You can also turn it on in the middle of weighing to lock the weigh when you feel it’s a better time (all you have to do just move the hanger and as soon as it sens a change in weigh it will go in locking mode in a couple of second.Tare function:This function is great because you can hang something (e.g. a bag) from this scale and tare it (e.g. zero the reading) to make the scale not account for that weight then add anything you want to it (e.g. the bag) and get the weigh of the item only.Hanger size:The hanger is a bit narrower for big international luggage but you can make it wider a bit with a plier OR just have a rope/chain with you that can go around the luggage handle then hang that to this hanger (and tare the wight of the rope/chain to get the accurate weigh). As for other things you can also hang any bag from the hanger (that fits the hanger) then put the items in the bag to figure out the weigh of them (again tare the bag weigh to get accurate reading).Accuracy:The scale is accurate I have compared it with other scale and got the same weigh.
