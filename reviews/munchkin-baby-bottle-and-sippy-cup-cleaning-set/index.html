Product Specifications

  • Baby bottle and sippy cup cleaning set includes (1) Sponge Bottle Brush, (1) Sprout Drying Rack and (1) Cleaning Brush Set
  • Cleaning Brush Set includes (2) straw brushes, (1) valve brush and (1) spout brush to help wash all of those hard-to-reach cup and bottle parts
  • Sponge is a bottle brush and nipple brush combined in one handy tool
  • Sprout drying rack includes built-in reservoir and spins for easy access

  • All brushes stand upright for quick drying, away from household germs

    Product Description

Size:6 Piece This Munchkin cleaning set has three convenient cleaning tools to help you keep all of your baby bottles and sippy cups clean and ready-to-use. Set includes one Sponge Bottle Brush, one Cleaning Brush Set, and one Sprout Drying Rack. Sponge is a 2-in-1 bottle brush and nipple brush, so you can clean baby bottles, sippy cups and more with just one convenient tool. The Cleaning Brush Set is a handy foursome of small brushes for washing all those hard-to-reach cup and bottle parts like valves, straws, and spouts - set includes 2 straw brushes, 1 valve brush, and 1 spout brush. Sponge and all the cleaning brushes have bases that stand upright on the countertop to allow them to dry quickly and away from germs. The Sprout drying rack offers a stylish and functional way to dry bottle and cup parts while keeping countertops clean and clutter-free. Sprout has a large 12 bottle capacity, a slotted drip tray, a built-in reservoir, and a spinning base.
