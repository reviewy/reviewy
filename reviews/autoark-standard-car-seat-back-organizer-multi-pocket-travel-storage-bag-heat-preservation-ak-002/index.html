Size:Standard - Organize your car and keep your backseat passengers happy with this convenient seatback storage bag.

  • Made of durable polyester, this organizer bag comes with enough pockets and compartments to help you stash and stow everything your passengers need to make it through any road trip in comfort.

Product Reviews

Major design flaws mean while sometimes useful, its mostly not.

I got this for a road trip and ended up barely using it because of 3 major design flaws that are not immediately noticeable until you take a trip with it. I am not saying its useless because it did have its uses and that’s why I am giving it 2 stars instead of 1. The towel or tissue holder on the bottom was great and it does give storage options. However here are the 3 drawbacks I found:1: The side net drink holders are actually fairly short, so taller drinks have a tendency to fall out with any sharp turns or sudden movements in a car. This means that in reality they are only useful for sealed shorter containers, and do NOT substitute as cup holders.2: The cooler bag component itself is fairly crappy quality. Its not sealed well, both at the top zipper point and further down. In fact mine arrived with a stitching issue that was not noticeable until I loaded the bag up the first time. Is it useful for keeping already cold items cold longer? Yes. But its very crappy and anything that could condense or leak, such as a cold pack, will be a problem and you could be left with water seeping out.3: The way it attaches to the seats of the car is going to be heavily dependent on your car type. In a smaller car it sticks out enough that it can be an issue for anyone in a back seat. In a larger car it can swing freely more easily causing a higher likelihood of things falling or spilling out. Its not exactly very sturdily attached to the vehicle no matter how you do it or what type of car you have. I found the best option being to turn it around the opposite of the picture and have it sitting down the front of the passenger seat. But that wont work if you are traveling with passengers.Buy at your own risk.
