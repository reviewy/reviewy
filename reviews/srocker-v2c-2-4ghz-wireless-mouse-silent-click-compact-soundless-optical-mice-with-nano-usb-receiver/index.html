The scroll wheel is silent, but will get noisier the faster you scroll.9. There is no on/off switch. To turn it on, plug in the receiver and click on any of the mouse buttons. It’ll automatically turn off once it’s idle.10. Sometimes the mouse would auto sleep while in use. Pretty annoying.11.
