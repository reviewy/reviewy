Product Specifications

  • (Includes 4pcs) Semi-Flexible: the panel can be flexed to a maximum of 30 degrees. This allows the panel to be flush mounted to curved surfaces like RV’s, sailboats, campers, tents, etc.
  • The solar panel and junction box are completely water resistant. Suitable for outdoor use in all weather conditions.
  • Extremely light weight and efficient. The solar panel weighs only 4 pounds and utilizes highly efficient (~ 24%) SunPower solar cells. The panel is easy to transport and mount, which is ideal for mobile applications.
  • Very easy to mount. Six evenly spaced metal grommet mounting holes are available to attach fasteners. The panel can also be mounting using adhesive or Velcro.

  • Blocking diode is included in the junction box to eliminate battery discharge/draining at night. The solar panel arrives ready for installation.
