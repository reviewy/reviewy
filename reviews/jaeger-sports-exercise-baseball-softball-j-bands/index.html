Great product for so many excerises!!

I just purchased this for my son who is a pitcher. I noticed his pitching coach was using this during his pitching workouts at the end. He would start out with a football, then several exercises with 2 different weighted heavy balls, throw live and always end up with these bands. His coach would have him do 7 different workouts with the bands, and when I asked about them he could not say enough good things about them. He told me for arm workouts he should use these every day, when getting ready to pitch, prior to taking the practice mound do a few specific exercises. The days after pitching in a game there are specific exercises he is to do to keep his arm flexible and strong. He also said that after an injury rehab therapists will use these type of bands to strengthen and work arm muscles, tendons etc. So many different things. My son was using a band he got from his High School trainer that is not close to the quality of this band so when his pitching coach recommend this one I got it. My son uses it every day!!
