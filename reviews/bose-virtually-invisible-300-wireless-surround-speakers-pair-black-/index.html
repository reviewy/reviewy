Product Specifications

  • The smallest speakers designed by Bose, they remain inconspicuous while delivering full, immersive sound-so the focus can be on your home entertainment, not your speaker.
  • Designed to be paired with the Sound Touch 300 sound bar and the Acoustic 300 wireless bass module to bring music, movies and TV to life with full, immersive surround sound.
  • Simply plug it in and enjoy your entertainment in minutes.
  • Connect wirelessly for easier placement, wherever you choose.

  • Dimensions/weight :Virtually Invisible 300 speakers (each):3.2” W x 3.7” H x 3.3” D (1.2 lbs); Power cord with adapter:6.54” W x 1.6” H x 3.07” D (9.8 oz)

    Product Description

It isn’t enough to enjoy the sound of your favorite music, movies, and TV shows. You want to be in the center of it all. You want to feel the car chases and explosions. You want to savor the guitar solos and sweeping verses. Designed to complement the Sound Touch 300 sound bar, Virtually Invisible 300 wireless surround speakers put you in the middle of the action. Virtually Invisible 300 surround speakers are the smallest speakers designed by Bose-so small they blend in with your Sound Touch 300 sound bar without taking too much space-so you can enjoy your home entertainment in full, immersive surround sound. Included: two Virtually Invisible 300 speakers; wireless surround speaker receivers; power supply; necessary cables.

I have the SoundTouch 300 soundbar and bought the surround speakers to fill out the sound. But they add very little to the listening experience. The audio that comes out is very very low. But as it is I have to put my ear right next to the speaker to even verify it’s working. Most of the time, even when watching movies, hbo, showtime, amc, very little is coming out of the surround speakers. When playing a surround sound test audio you get something, but blu rays, streaming, cable not much. If only there were a volume control on the surround speakers it would be acceptable. Poor speakers, not recommended.
