200 Watts of Greatness!

It’s Samsung and it’s great. Since the ad doesn’t state it I’m posting this letting you know it has 200 Watts and it’s a sweet spot. I was between this sound bar or Samsung HW-J355 (120 watts) or LG LAS465B (300 watts).I didn’t like that J355 only had 120 watts and I didn’t like the design of the LAS465B (it looks cheap, speakers are visible and it just screams “LOOK AT ME! I AM A SOUND BAR AND I HAVE 300 WATTS!”).Besides, this sweet soundbar has an awesome WIRELESS sub-woofer (that’s right, no cables between the sound bar and the sub-woofer at all) so I was able to put this soundbar under my 60 inch sharp TV via Digital Audio cable (which came included in the box, another bonus) and the sub-woofer right next to the sofa for some great bass and amplified sound. The display is easy to see and understand, remote looks and works great (looks the same as a Samsung curve TV remote) and the soundbar + sub-woofer look very professional and very well-designed.This soundbar turns on when I turn my TV on and turns off a few minutes after I turn the TV off as long as the source is on digital audio (this would be probably be seamless if I had a Samsung-compatible TV but it still works great nonetheless).Finally, I paired my Amazon Echo with this soundbar for extra awesomeness. What’s even cooler is that if I say “Alexa, turn on Bluetooth” it actually turns on the soundbar for me, allowing me to enjoy music I love with great sound. When I say “Alexa turn off Bluetooth” it disconnects from the soundbar and in a short while the soundbar also turns off - nearly no need for the remote.I am enjoying this sound bar to the fullest and I highly recommend it :)
