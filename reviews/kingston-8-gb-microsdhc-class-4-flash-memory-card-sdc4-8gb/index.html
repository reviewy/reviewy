    Product Description

Package Type:Standard Packaging Starting at 4GB, microSDHC cards offer higher storage for more music, more videos, more pictures, more games, more of everything you need in today’s mobile world. The microSDHC card allows you to maximize today’s revolutionary mobile devices. Kingston’s microSDHC cards use the new speed class rating of Class 4 that guarantee a minimum data transfer rate of 4MB/ sec. for optimum performance with devices that use microSDHC. Identical in physical size to today’s standard microSD card, the microSDHC cards are designed to comply with SD Specification Version 2.00 and are only recognized by microSDHC host devices. They can be used as full-size SDHC cards when used with the included adapter. To ensure compatibility, look for the microSDHC or SDHC logo on host devices (e.g., phones, PDAs and cameras). Wherever you find yourself in the mobile world, you can rely on Kingston’s microSDHC cards. All cards are 100-percent tested and are backed by a lifetime warranty and 24⁄7 live technical support.

Works great with Roku 2⁄3

This Kingston 4 GB microSDHC Class 4 Flash Memory Card works great with the Roku 2 or Roku 3 Media Player/Streamers and improve their channel/game load times. You only need a 2 GB as that is even more than the Rokus can actually use. This is the least expensive micro SD card that I found that met the Roku specification, which is: 2GB-32GB size range and speed class 2 or 4 or 6. Larger or Faster cards do not work at all or do not work reliably. The Roku’s can’t handle a class 10 or higher speed at all and if accepted will not be reliable. And again the size needed is only 2GB as to what the Roku 2⁄3 can use, but if you have an old one laying around or in can purchase one less expensive with larger storage up to 32GB they will work. Just use or purchase the least expensive micro SD card you can that meets the specs.
