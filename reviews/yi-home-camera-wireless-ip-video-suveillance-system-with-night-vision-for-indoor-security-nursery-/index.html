If you already have one of these DO NOT UPDATE TO 1.8.6.1B_201604071151 firmware!!.*UPDATE 4⁄28 I did get a response back finally from support, they sent me the previous firmware, and I uploaded it to both cameras and now the night vision is sharp again. I attached two photos from the same camera, you can clearly see the loss of detail, exposure and usefullness that firmware 201604071151 caused. The working firmware they gave me is 201601071352 and I’m happy with this older version.
