Nice but could be more stable..

I like having the sockets on the side so the plugs don’t stick out. The option of having one side swivel is also a nice feature if you have something a little larger to plug in that won’t rest against the wall although that might be an issue it that cord was heavy. I especially like the 2 USB ports, which is one of the reasons I purchased this item. The only negative thing I have to say is that it could be more stable in the outlet. You do have to hold on to it if you need to unplug anything including something plugged into the USP port. I don’t think it would be a good product if the outlets were on the front as the weight of the cords plugged into it would pull it out of the outlet. It would be nice if the unit could be made with a hole and a long screw to actually attach it to the outlet. That would stabilize it. For me it isn’t much of an issue because I never or very rarely unplug anything from it as it is behind one of my end tables..
