Looks very nice. Pretty good functionality. This is a plastic gun, cheap, but serves the function for which it was purchased.

For the money (less than $15) it’s a great little shooter. However, it shoots very low. So low in fact it has been impossible to adjust the “scope” to get near the impact. About 10’ is the limit to try and hit something using the “scope.” The plastic “iron sights” are pretty good and work out to about 20’ not too bad, but still shoots very low. Gun is all plastic and you can feel it when you rack the slide. I hit it with a little dry spray lube and it seems to help. Clip works very well, holds about 14 bbs. It is possible to put in backwards (children). Adults can easily figure it out. BBs do not come out that fast. Goes thru paper targets fine, but thick “paper board” is a challenge. Most of the time it just embeds itself. Won’t dent a tin can but will dent a soda can. Laser is okay, but mount broke because I was trying to tighten it to keep it from wobbling and one of the mounting edges broke. (found a solution online using a small pipe moutning clamp called and SMC (15mm) at flexpvc.) The switch for the laser is another matter. No matter how I tried to use the included elastic band thing it really never works. The wires gets in the way and I end up holding it with my finger wrapped around the grip and try not get tangled in the wire.Don’t expect it to last very long, but for an early training pistol for children it’s pretty good. Really does look good too.
