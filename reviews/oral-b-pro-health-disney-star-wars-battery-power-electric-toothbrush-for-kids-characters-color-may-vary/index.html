Great but loud and large

Â These are a great quality little electric toothbrush for a child. We were slightly bummed when we ordered 2 (one for each child) and got the same character (yoda). We solved that issue by writing their initials on the bottom in sharpie marker & it’s been fine (thankfully they have different first initials!!).We got these to replace the kid’s old Colgate electric toothbrushes and there were a few things I was quite pleased about, however there were also some things we also had some major “growing pains” to get through as well. We loved that for such an inexpensive brush, the bristles do actually rotate! That also means those little openings in the back of the toothbrush for water to get in. For our family, it’s not a huge issue since our children aren’t able to brush their own teeth, so that means a parent is rinsing out and drying the brushes after each use (and inspecting for potential areas where water could hide out and feed mold). I did want to mention it, though, so that other parents remember to check your little ones brushes and teach them good brush hygiene. ;) Also, another “issue” that wasn’t a problem for our family was the handle size on the brush. It’s significantly larger than any other “kids” toothbrush that we have used. If you have a small 3 or 4 year old, it may take a bit of getting used to at first. The biggest problem that I see with these toothbrushes is the noise! We’ve been using them for about 2.5 months now and our children use them AWESOME now, but at first these brushes scared them! It took about one and a half weeks for our kids to get back into a regular “no big deal” schedule of brushing their teeth. At first they pushed it away as soon as you turned the brush on! If you have kids with pronounced auditory sensitivities- these would be a hard pass. But if you have kids that might have “slight sound aversions”, don’t automatically put these on your “NO” list. Our kids only took 1.5 weeks to get them added into their daily schedule (huge win for kiddos with special needs).
